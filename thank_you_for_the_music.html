Thank You for the Music

ABBA  ·  Fill in the blanks
Interactive exercise by Roman Samoylov
Instructions: Listen to the song 2–3 times and fill in the missing words. Each box represents one letter — use the number of boxes as a hint. After typing a letter the cursor moves automatically. All choruses are linked — filling one fills the others automatically. The ? button next to each line reveals one random missing letter — but hinted letters don't count toward your score. Press Check when you're done.
