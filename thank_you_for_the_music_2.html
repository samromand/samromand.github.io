About the Song
Thank You for the Music is a song by Swedish pop group ABBA, released in 1977 on the album The Album.

Famous for their crystal-clear pronunciation and irresistible melodies, ABBA became one of the best-selling music acts of all time — making their songs perfect for English listening practice.
